Frequently Asked Questions

What can I store?

Common household goods, clothing, sports equipment (snorkel gear, surfboards), seasonal items, and personal belongings of moderate value. NOT for heirlooms, jewelry, cryptocurrency wallets, or items you cannot afford to lose.

How does prepayment work?

Pay upfront for 1-4 months. Retrieve early? Get a prorated refund. Retrieve late? Pay additional months at standard rate. 3-day grace period after prepaid period ends.

Is this for frequent access?

No. This service is designed for longer absences (weeks to months), not regular access. Retrieval fees ($10 each) add up quickly if you need frequent access.

What if I don't pay?

After 30 days of non-payment, items are considered abandoned and we can take possession, sell, donate, or discard them.
