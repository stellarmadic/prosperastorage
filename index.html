Frequently Asked Questions

How does pricing work?

You pay upfront for your chosen storage duration (1-4 months). If you retrieve early, you get a prorated refund. If you retrieve late, you pay for the additional months.

What can I store?

Common household goods, clothing, sports equipment (snorkel gear, surfboards, jiu-jitsu mats), seasonal items, and personal belongings. We cannot store valuables, heirlooms, cryptocurrency wallets, hazardous materials, or perishables.

How do I get my items back?

Simply contact us via WhatsApp or email to schedule delivery. We'll bring your boxes to your door at your scheduled time. $10 retrieval fee applies.

Is there a minimum storage period?

Minimum charge is 1 month, but we recommend 3-4 months for best value compared to checked luggage costs.

How secure is the storage?

Your items are stored in a secure, climate-controlled facility maintained at 80¬∞F with dehumidification. We carry insurance and maintain restricted access.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards and cryptocurrency via Stripe. Payment is required upfront when booking storage.
